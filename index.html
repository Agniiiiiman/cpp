The Universe is everything we can touch, feel, sense, measure or detect. It includes all of space, time, matter, and energy. The Universe began with the Big Bang approximately 13.8 billion years ago and has been expanding ever since.

Galaxies, stars, planets, black holes, dark matter, and dark energy are all part of the Universe. There are over 100 billion galaxies in the observable universe alone!
